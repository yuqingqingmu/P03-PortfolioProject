Graphic - Poster Design

About

This is the final project for IAT 103 in 2019 which is about holding a feasible campaign for introducing the idea of avoiding plagiarism to first year students. The biggest part for this project is to design a serial of posters to get attention from the target audience and let them take part in our campaign.

My Role

Major designer and illustrator

APPLIED SKILLS/TOOLS

Adobe Illstruator, Indesign, Photoshop, Procreate

Process

Step 1 - Research and Color Pallete

First, we have researched the relative poster, and decided the style as a team. Since we were creating a serial of posters, so we were also looking for the bright color pallete to match our theme - party.

Step 2 - Sketching

As the major designer, I started to draw the poster sketch and decided the visual elements. In this stage, I was still not deciding how many illustration should I put on the poster, then I created two different layout in order to discuss with my team mates later.

Step 3 - Digital Draft

Some of my group members prefered the simple and clear layout compared with the repeating typography, then I tried to use photoShop to visualize the idea: multiple illustration and the title is simple and clean with all the necessary information

Step 4 - Final

This is the final stage of the process. There are few things that I improve from the last setp

  • Using the illustrator to create the final visual elements: phone and pencil
  • Deciding the final typography basing on the feedback
  • Adding the Chinese on the corner in order to attract target audience

Challenge & Solution

Since we are creating a serial of posters, it is hard to unify the design style when we have five designer in a group.

Solution

In order to solve the problem, I communicated with my team members to decide what kinds of elements should be included on the poster, and asked everybody to research some related posters. After researching and brainstorming, we all agreed to use the studentâ€™s daily item to symbolize individuals, and use bright color palette to create the posters. As the main designer, I was in charge of create the first layout and the illustration, then my teammates could follow the template to make the same style posters.

Reflection

I learned a lot by doing the project with my teammates. The first thing is we should analyzing the target audience, then we will know what is kind of poster will get their attention in order to run a campaign. Secondly, typography layout could be also important besides the illustration, a good layoutr could make the theme clear.
